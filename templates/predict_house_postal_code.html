Predict House Price with Postal Code

Features
  1. postal_code (str): Postal code of city

  2. number_of_rooms (int): The number of rooms of the property

  3. house_area (int): The area (m2) of the house (floors)

  4. fully_equipped_kitchen (str): If the house has a fully equipped kitchen or not

  5. open_fire (str): If there's an open fire or not

  6. terrace (str): If there's a terrace or not

  7. garden (str): If there's a garden or not

  8. surface_of_the_land (int): The area (m2) of the land

  9. number_of_facadess (int): The number of facades (0 to 4)

  10. swimming_pool (str): If there's a swimming pool or not

  11. state_of_the_building (str): as new/good/just renovated/to renovated/unknown

  12. construction_year (int): The property built's year

Postal codes correspondence to Region/Province
  • 1000–1299 : Région de Bruxelles-Capitale

  • 1300–1499 : Province du Brabant wallon

  • 1500–1999 : Province du Brabant flamand (arrondissement de Hal-Vilvorde, sauf Overijse)

  • 2000–2999 : Province d'Anvers

  • 3000–3499 : Province du Brabant flamand (arrondissement de Louvain, plus Overijse)

  • 3500–3999 : Province de Limbourg

  • 4000–4999 : Province de Liège

  • 5000–5999 : Province de Namur

  • 6000–6599 : Province de Hainaut (1)

  • 6600–6999 : Province de Luxembourg

  • 7000–7999 : Province de Hainaut (2)

  • 8000–8999 : Province de Flandre-Occidentale

  • 9000–9999 : Province de Flandre-Orientale
